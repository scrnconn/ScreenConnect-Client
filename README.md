## Introduction

ScreenConnect Client is a lightweight application that lets users securely join and participate in remote support or remote access sessions powered by ConnectWise ScreenConnect. It is designed for quick installation and fast connection, so a technician or authorized support specialist can view your screen, troubleshoot issues, and guide you through steps in real time. With ScreenConnect Client, you can share your desktop on demand or allow unattended access (when configured by your organization) for ongoing maintenance, updates, and monitoring.

The client supports essential remote-assistance features such as screen viewing and control, chat for communication during the session, and convenient tools like file transfer to exchange documents, logs, or installers. Depending on the permissions granted, support staff can reboot the computer and reconnect, adjust session settings, or run diagnostics to speed up problem resolution. Security is a key focus: connections are typically protected with encryption, and access can be limited through authentication, role-based permissions, and session logging to help organizations meet internal compliance requirements.

ScreenConnect Client is commonly used by IT teams, managed service providers, and help desks to reduce downtime, avoid unnecessary site visits, and deliver consistent support to employees and customers. It is a practical solution for both one-time troubleshooting and ongoing remote device management.
